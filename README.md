## Buyer checklist

- Category first, product second.
- Treat coupons as a bonus, not a reason to buy.
- Estimate shipping before the haul gets heavy.
- Compare QC photos against the product promise.
- Skip finds that need perfect luck to make sense.

Independent guide. Product listings, prices, QC options, agent availability, and shipping details may change.
