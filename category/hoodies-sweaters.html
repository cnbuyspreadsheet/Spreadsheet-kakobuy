Fit and use

Start by deciding whether the item belongs in a daily haul, a single statement piece, or a risky experiment. Spreadsheet browsing gets messy when every find looks urgent. For hoodies & sweaters, the useful question is simple: would you still want it after shipping cost, QC delay, and possible size uncertainty are included?

Proof to inspect

  • Compare title, images, and available size notes.
  • Check color and material clues before assuming retail-like texture.
  • Look for measurements or comments that reduce sizing guesswork.
  • Use QC photos after purchase to confirm shape, stitching, print position, and obvious defects.
