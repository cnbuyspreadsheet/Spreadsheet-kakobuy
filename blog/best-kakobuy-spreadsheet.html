What matters

Spreadsheet browsing works best when it stays boring. A row is only a lead, not proof. Before ordering, compare the title, images, size notes, expected weight, seller clues, and the live product page. If too many details require optimism, skip the find and choose one with clearer evidence. This saves more money than chasing a tiny discount or trusting a viral screenshot. QC photos are the second gate: check shape, color, stitching, print placement, measurements, and obvious defects before international shipping. The live product page is where current prices, agent availability, product options, and route details belong, because static guide pages cannot keep those facts fresh every hour.
